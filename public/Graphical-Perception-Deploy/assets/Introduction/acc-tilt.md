## Perception of Tilt (<span style="color:red"> Ignore all elements except for the Tilt </span>)

- You are about to see two black lines forming a tilt.  
- Please answer **how many degrees** the line is tilted **compared to the horizontal line**.
- The tilt is measured **counter clockwise** from the **right horizontal line**.
- The answer should be a number between **0° ~ 180°**.
