## Perception of Curvatures (<span style="color:red"> Ignore all elements except for the Curvature </span>)

- You are about to see a curve connecting two points.  
- Please answer **how much the line is curved**.
- Curvature is measured as the central angle formed by connecting the endpoints of the curve to the center of the corresponding circle.
- The answer should be a number **between 0 and 180**.
